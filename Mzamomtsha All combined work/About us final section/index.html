About Us

Mission

Mzamomtsha Primary School at Mfuleni, Driftsands, Western Cape. Our mission is to develop students with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical. Mzamomtsha Primary School is a public primary school. In 2014 there were 293 registered learners, including 51 Grade R learners. in 2010 we manage to get 100% pass rate in all our grades. By the of 2013 we received a sponsorship from Powerade as we were amongst the best athletes in Western Cape Our Rugby Team became the winners of the Western Cape School Rugby Tournaments in 2019

Vision

At Mzamomtsha Primary we strive for excellence and technological expansion as our school grows in this new aged world. Our vision for the future is to upgrade the facilities at our school to ensure all our children have equal opportunities in a world where technology rules. We want them to be equipped with relevant skills and a mind that can understand the new expansions within the technological field. Mzamomtsha Primary ensures all employed staff are catered to their jobs in highest capability to ensure they push the students to their highest potential. We supply an open and safe environment to the children to ensure the diversity and individuality of future employees. Our school implements a system of values per grade to ensure the children learn valuable life values through yearly activities that are able to be used to their benefit in future work environments. We strive for academic achievements as we have included compulsory after-school classes for the struggling students to achieve at their maximum capability.
